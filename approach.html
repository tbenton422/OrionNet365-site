How we work

Assess → Stabilize → Operate → Improve.

We focus on repeatable outcomes. That means clear scope, measurable baselines, and practical controls your team can sustain.

1) Assess

We establish a baseline across operations, identity, security, and governance.

  • Environment discovery and risk review
  • Tooling assessment (Defender, Sentinel, M365)
  • Operational pain points and stakeholder goals

2) Stabilize

Quick wins that reduce incidents and improve visibility—without boiling the ocean.

  • Alert tuning and escalation paths
  • Identity hardening and access controls
  • Backup verification and recovery checks

3) Operate

Runbooks, change management, and reporting that keep the environment steady.

  • Patch and maintenance cadence
  • Incident response playbooks
  • Monthly operational and security reporting

4) Improve

Once the environment is stable, we implement automation and continuous improvement cycles to reduce manual burden and strengthen security posture over time.

Automation

AI-assisted triage, scripted remediation, and repeatable workflows that reduce Tier-1 load.

Security maturity

Detection use cases, log retention, and policy alignment as your environment evolves.

Roadmaps

Quarterly planning cycles tied to risk reduction and stakeholder priorities.

Delivery style is tailored to your environment: infrastructure projects often use hybrid waterfall + sprint execution; security operations uses iterative improvement cycles with clear metrics.
